MOBILE AUGMENTED REALITY SYSTEM (MARS)

AR systems are studied for over 20 years. With smartphone are ubiquitous, mobile AR systems (MARS) open various opportunities for users to experience and interact with physical location information on the move (Kourouthanassis, Boletsis, Bardaki, & Chasanidou, 2015).
